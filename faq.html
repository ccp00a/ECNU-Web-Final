Frequently ask Questions

  • Will my college or university have access to what I learn on CsCourse？

    • If your college or university decides to participate in CsCourse, your learning information will be visible to the administrators from your college or university that are managing the program.

  • What can I do if I am a high school student or not currently enrolled in a university?

  • Is customer support free?

    • Yes. There are no customer support charges.
